undo / redo

Every action chain and every direct edit of a control creates an undo step. Rapid edits of the same node (e.g. typing) are merged into one step. Use the buttons or - since this form has the keyboard-shortcuts attribute - Ctrl/Cmd+Z and Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Z.
